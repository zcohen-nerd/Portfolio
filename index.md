## About Me

![Zachary Cohen Professional Headshot]({{ '/assets/images/zachary-cohen-headshot.jpg' | relative_url }}){:loading="lazy"}

I am an **Electromechanical engineer** specializing in **systems-level mechatronics, PCB design, and engineering education**. My work bridges research, design, and instruction — developing integrated hardware systems that teach as they perform. With experience spanning additive manufacturing, control systems, and digital electronics, I focus on transforming conceptual designs into fully realized, documented systems.

Currently, I support the **Weapons, Robotics, and Control Engineering Department at the U.S. Naval Academy**, where I design and implement electromechanical testbeds and educational tools that empower students to explore robotics, automation, and embedded design. My work emphasizes **system integration, documentation standards, and design reproducibility**, helping future engineers understand both how and why complex systems work.

Outside of the Academy, I teach **Solid Modeling with SolidWorks** at Anne Arundel Community College, mentor engineering students on capstone and independent projects, and continuously expand my own practice in PCB design, systems engineering, and advanced manufacturing. My design philosophy is simple: **build precisely, document thoroughly, and teach openly.**
