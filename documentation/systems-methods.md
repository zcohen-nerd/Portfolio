## What this section covers

This page captures the methods I repeatedly use when planning and integrating multidisciplinary engineering work.

## Core Method Areas

- Requirements framing and clarification
- Interface definition and dependency mapping
- Risk identification and mitigation planning
- Verification planning and evidence capture
- Post-iteration retrospectives and process improvement

## Practical intent

These methods are described as reusable workflow patterns, not as formal guarantees of project outcomes. They are adapted to project size, risk profile, and team structure.

## Evidence posture

Case-specific quantitative outcomes are excluded from this page unless the supporting artifacts are publicly shareable.
