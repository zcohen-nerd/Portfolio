## What this section covers

This page captures methods used when planning and integrating multidisciplinary engineering work.

## Core Method Areas

- Requirements framing and clarification
- Interface definition and dependency mapping
- Risk identification and mitigation planning
- Verification planning and evidence capture
- Post-iteration retrospectives and process improvement

## Practical intent

These methods are described as reusable workflow patterns, not as guarantees of project outcomes.
