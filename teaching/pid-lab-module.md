## Module Purpose

This module moves students from abstract control concepts to practical engineering judgment through repeated observe-model-tune-reflect cycles.

## Instructional Design Priorities

- Link theory to physical response
- Support safe lab execution and reproducible setup
- Emphasize technical communication and documentation
- Increase difficulty from guided labs to independent tuning decisions

## Instructor Contribution

- Developed and iterated lab sequence
- Built and maintained hardware-instruction alignment
- Integrated rubric-based feedback for technical reasoning and documentation quality

## Accuracy note

Public-facing outcomes are intentionally qualitative unless accompanied by clearly scoped cohort data.
