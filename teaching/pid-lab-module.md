## Module Purpose

This module is designed to move students from abstract control concepts to practical engineering judgment through repeated observe-model-tune-reflect cycles.

## Instructional Design Priorities

- Clear linkage between theory and physical response
- Safe lab execution and reproducible setup
- Strong emphasis on technical communication and documentation
- Scaffolded difficulty from guided labs to independent tuning decisions

## Instructor Contribution

- Developed and iterated lab sequence
- Built/maintained hardware-instruction alignment
- Integrated rubric-based feedback for technical reasoning and documentation quality
