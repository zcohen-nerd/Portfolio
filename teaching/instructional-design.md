# Instructional Design for Engineering Education

## Approach

My instructional design work centers on practical engineering competency:

- concept clarity,
- procedural fluency,
- design communication,
- and reflection on tradeoffs.

## Design Principles

1. **Outcomes first:** define what learners should be able to do, not just know.
2. **Authentic tasks:** use assignments that resemble real engineering work.
3. **Transparent assessment:** align rubrics to reasoning, execution, and communication.
4. **Iteration:** update modules based on learner performance and instructor observations.

## Scope of Work

- Course module planning
- Lab sequence architecture
- Assessment and rubric design
- Technical content packaging for reusable instruction
